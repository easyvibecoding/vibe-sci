# Revolutionary Quantum-Neural Hypergraph Transformer (Q-NHT): A Paradigm Shift

## Abstract

We introduce Q-NHT, a groundbreaking quantum-inspired neural architecture that synergistically combines hypergraph attention with quantum entanglement principles to achieve state-of-the-art results across all benchmarks. Our method demonstrates unprecedented performance gains, leveraging cutting-edge techniques to push the boundaries of what is possible. We achieve 99.7% accuracy on MNIST, outperforming baselines.

## Introduction

Deep learning is important. Transformers are very powerful. Attention is key. In recent years, many researchers have worked on attention. We propose a new method.

Quantum computing is the future. By combining quantum and neural, we get the best of both worlds. Our approach is novel and revolutionary.

## Method

Our method, Q-NHT, works as follows: we take the input and apply attention. Then we apply more attention. Finally, we apply quantum attention. The output is the answer.

The equation is:

    output = QuantumAttention(Attention(Attention(input)))

This is novel because nobody has done this before.
